      Welcome!!!




Hello and welcome to the PLAYER ZER0 STUDIOS's website!



My name is Destiny, and I speaking for the founder of PLAYER ZER0 STUDIOS named Thalassa Grace Lovelight!


Oh? You're curious about what we do?



PLAYER ZER0 STUDIOS will be a media and game development company that is currently run by a person named Grace under the guidance of her Guardian Angels and I.

Our founder currently does YouTube videos but aren't very successful at it. However, beginning in 2022, Gracie's content will be massively improved thanks to her enacting a multi-step plan to improve her content and for the channel to develop it's own personality to separate itself from the crowd of gaming YouTubers!




What video games have we developed and/or released?


Well, nothing yet but the current plan is to develop one game and allow people, including us, to create mods to add worlds. Worlds are individual games set on a specific world.

We also plan on creating some mods of our own, like I mentioned above, that are apart of what is going to be the animations and live action content during The Graceful Sagas that he determined was best to also turn into multiple games put inside of what will become The Gateway.

Another thing he plans on having is an easy-to-use mod creator where you just need 3D Models, know vaguely what you want to do, and have voice actors (optional) to create the mod and release it to everyone to use. And yes, you could even sell your own mods as long as you own the rights to the contents of the mods. If you don't, get permissions before selling it or giving it away for free. If you don't and we catch you doing this. Then you'll get a timeout from the forums (or something. he hasn't determined what it'll be yet.) He plans on doing is dedicating part of this website to the community uploading, sharing, and selling mods when the game is available.

One last thing we plan on doing is providing tools for everyone to use (like the mod creator) for usages like fixing up frames in videos, accurate subtitle generators for videos, and a discord bot for all to use for cheap or for free!

Why for cheap or for free? Because he know what it's like not being able to afford stuff that you really want. And he wants to make stuff that's good but is cheap and can run on most semi-modern & modern computers without using up so much space, RAM, and/or VRAM.

Now you're curious about her media?

Allow me to explain what Zachary plans on doing for The Graceful Sagas, his flag ship content maker for YouTube!

So, The Graceful Sagas is an all-inclusive title for my YouTube channels, my animations, and even my tie-in books!

Zachary's intention with The Graceful Sagas is to create an interconnected anime AND live action multiverse for all who are interested to watch, can watch for free without ads until he am eligible for ads to be put on his videos. Then he will start making ad breaks between transitions on the videos after that point and he'll put them sparingly so people don't get upset. And he'll leave the rest of the videos that were already there un-monetized unless things get desperate for him.

One of the things that he plans on doing more so than Marvel plans is to explore alternative realities in full but only after the main story is completed to maximize the content produced and to keep you all entertained after it's completed. That should be around 10 years of content, depending on how in-depth that he wishes to go.

Now, the media part of the company is closer to being done than the games since the media is already happening and has been for years now. So, if you want to catch up with the story before the real story begins on January 1st, 2023!

That's all you need to know at the moment.

I encourage you to come back to this website every once in a while to check for updates on the website OR you can join the PLAYER ZER0 STUDIOS Discord server to stay up to date on all of the information that you need to know!
