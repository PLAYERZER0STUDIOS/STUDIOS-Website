Dear Toontown
I heard there used to be a merry-go-round in Minnie's Melodyland. Is that true???
- Josh
Dear Josh,

Yes that is true! In the early days of Toontown Minnie's Melodyland played host to a merry-go-round in the middle of the playground that Toons could ride. When Fishing was introduced,
each playground needed a fishing hole. The best location in Minnie's Melodyland was right in the middle, so the Toon Council decided to remove the merry-go-round to
make room for the fishing pond. However, ever since the merry-go-round was removed, Toons have asked for the return of this long lost bit of Toon fun. We hope this clears up the mystery of the merry-go-round!
Your Friends at Toontown
